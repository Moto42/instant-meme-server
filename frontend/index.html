How to use

What have you done?

Instant-Meme is a service that lets you build text-on-image 'memes' by quickly describing them in the URL linking to the image.

Why did you do this?

Building memes on a website or in photoshopt gets the job done, but it pulls you out of the conversation for to long when you just want to drop the image in chat. When you have a great idea for a good joke, a five minute delay just doesn't work..

How does this work?

To define a custom meme on-the-fly, you just link to it with the following format

https://instant-meme.herokuapp.com/meme-name.png?t1=first textblock&t2=second text

Heres a part by part breakdown of the above

Click to open

  • https://instant-meme.herokuapp.com/

    Just the url pointing to the Instant-Meme server. Nothing special so far

  • meme-name.png

    The name of the meme template you wish to use. You can see a full list of the available temlates below.

  • ?

    Required control character. The question mark tells the server where the address ends, and your options begin.

  • t1=first textblock

    The first text block.
    The t1= identifies it as the first code block.
    The & tells the server that this is the end of this block, and another one it following.

  • t2=second text

    The final block of text. Again, it is identified as the second block by the t2=. No & is needed after this one, because there will be nothing following it.
